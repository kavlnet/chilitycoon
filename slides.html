(and knowing when to quit)

Karl Yang ‚Äî Rippling International Payroll

The Setup

  • Deel was already in market with 150+ countries
  • We were late. The obvious move: match their coverage.
  • But we didn't have years to catch up.

Our Secret

  • "Global payroll" is a misnomer
  • 90% of the market is in a handful of countries
  • Deel built for the checkbox. We'd build for reality.
  • If we're 10x better where it matters, we win.

What "Deep" Meant

Rippling (deep) Deel (broad)
Build payroll + EOR in each country EOR only, aggregate payroll partners
Hourly workers, flexible pay schedules Standardized across countries
Vertical integration Stitch together partners

We explicitly decided not to play Deel's game
